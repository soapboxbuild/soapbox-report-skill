This Decarbonization Capital Plan presents a phased investment program to systematically reduce the subject property's carbon intensity, eliminate near-term CRREM stranding risk, and deliver measurable financial returns through energy cost savings and enhanced asset positioning. Measures are sequenced by tier — prioritizing investments with the strongest risk-adjusted returns in early years, followed by strategic deeper retrofits that position the asset for long-term regulatory compliance. All figures are pre-tax unless otherwise noted; IRR and NPV include modeled incentive proceeds.
