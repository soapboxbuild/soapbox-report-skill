# Stoneweg US Logo

Place the Stoneweg US logo here as `logo.svg` (SVG format preferred, minimum 200px wide).

Until `logo.svg` is present in this directory, the renderer will fall back to the Soapbox default logo.

**File:** `logo.svg`
**Format:** SVG (preferred) or PNG
**Minimum width:** 200px
**Recommended:** white or transparent background for use on the deep navy (`#1B2A3B`) header
